Paneer masala curry

  1. Take cashews,tomatoes,onions and make them into a paste
  2. Take a pan and add oil,whole masala and mix these paste into it
  3. Heat it for about 5 mins and then add paneer cubes
  4. Finally add some cream and heat it about 10mins and dish was ready
